## BLISSFUL NIGERIA PLC
### FINANCIAL INSIGHT ANALYSIS FOR YEAR 2023

### Executive Summary

The Financial Insight Dashboard provides a concise overview of Blissful Nigeria PLC financial performance and position as at 31 December 2023. It consolidates key financial metrics from the Statement of Profit or Loss (SPL), Statement of Financial Position (SOFP), and Statement of Changes in Equity (SOCE), enabling management to evaluate profitability, liquidity, and financial stability at a glance.


### Profitability Analysis

Blissful Nigeria PLC generated ₦4.03 billion in revenue during the reporting period, indicating a relatively strong market presence and healthy business activity. The company generated revenue, resulting in a Gross Profit of ₦753 million,  which represents a Gross Profit Margin of 18.68%. This indicates that the business retained approximately 19 kobo from every ₦1 of revenue after covering direct production or operating costs.

EBITDA stood at ₦318 million, translating to an EBITDA Margin of 7.89%. This reflects the company's operating profitability before accounting for financing costs, taxes, depreciation, and amortization.

The company recorded a Net Profit of ₦107 million, demonstrating that it remained profitable after accounting for all operating and non-operating expenses.

### Financial Position Analysis

Blissful Nigeria PLC reported Total Assets of ₦534 million, with a strong cash balance of ₦267 million, indicating good short-term liquidity.

Accounts Receivable amounted to ₦148 million, suggesting that a significant portion of sales remains outstanding for collection. Efficient receivables management will be important to maintain healthy cash flows.

Payables stood at ₦138 million, representing the company's outstanding obligations to suppliers and creditors.

### Equity Analysis

Shareholders' Equity was ₦396 million, representing 74.23% of Total Assets. This high Equity Ratio indicates that the company finances a substantial portion of its assets through shareholders' funds rather than debt, reflecting a relatively strong capital structure and lower financial risk.

The equity base consists of:

- Share Capital: ₦10 million

- Share Premium: ₦200 million

- Retained Earnings: ₦186 million

The equity structure demonstrates a financially stable organization.

Retained earnings balance suggests that the company has accumulated profits over time, which can support future growth and expansion.

### Overall Assessment

Overall, the financial dashboard portrays a company with:

- Strong revenue generation.

- Positive profitability across all profit levels.

- A healthy equity position with limited reliance on external financing.

- Adequate cash resources to support operations.

- Opportunities to improve working capital through efficient receivables management.

### Areas for Improvement

- Improve operating efficiency to convert more gross profit into net profit.
  
- Continue managing administrative and financing costs.
  
- Increase EBITDA margin through cost optimization.
  
- Enhance profit retention for future expansion.
